Chapter 6

"It's no problem" Aiden said, before looking at his phone. "Shit, I'm late, I need to meet up with the guys..."

"O-oh...well, don't let me get in your way then"

"Nah, it's fine. I liked today." Aiden said before adding softly "you look good, by the way"

Marin's face flushed scarlet.

"I-i look perfectly normal. No different from yesterday. W-why compliment me all of a sudden-" Marin started, rambling as his eyes darted about, looking at anything except Aiden's eyes.

Aiden just chuckled before patting Marin's head. "Yeah, well I think you look better today. See you later, glowstick" he said teasingly, before leaving.

Marin stood there for a while, cheeks still burning, before sighing.

'i think I'm starting to trust him'

Marin thought to himself, leaning against the wall.

'its risky... dangerous, even'

He paused. Looked in the direction Aiden had left in.

And smiled genuinely for the first time in a long time.

'i think I like it'.

And with that thought, the butterflies in his stomach intensified.

The bell suddenly rang, snapping Marin out of his thoughts

As he walked to class, he caught a glimpse of Aiden and his friends.

Marin suddenly felt inferior.

'I'll never be as free as they are...and besides...Aiden's a popular kid...he probably just hangs out with me out of pity...'

Marin thought to himself as he walked into his classroom

He sat in the back, as usual.

Unnoticed.

Silent.

Focused on the lesson.

But somewhere, deep inside him...

His heart kept fluttering.

By closing time, Marin once again found himself in the library.

'Ethan isn't here yet...' Marin thought to himself, looking up from the book he was reading at the time.

He sighed.

stared out the window.

Got lost in thought

Till he heard a voice.

"Marin?"

He stilled.

He knew who that was.

Maddie.

He didn't know why, but her presence made him a bit uncomfortable.

She took a seat in front of him, a huge grin on her face, her fangs glinting.

"I was hopping I'd find you here. Why're you all alone?" She said, propping her chin up on her hand.

"W-well... I was just waiting for Ethan... We recently became friends..." Marin said

"Oh, Ethan Rondava? That nerd? THAT'S who you're friends with?" Maddie said, her voice taking on a mocking tone.

Marin didn't like her tone.

It didn't sit right with him.

"... Are you saying I SHOULDN'T be friends with Ethan....?"

"No, no, no, I didn't mean it like THAT, I just mean...you could do better, y'know?"

"...are you saying... Ethan isn't good enough to be my friend?"

"Yeah. You could do WAY better"

"Ok, who do you think I should be friends with, then?"

"I dunno. Popular people. like ME."

"I don't really like you popular people. You guys are so full of yourself. Most of you have this 'I'm better than you' attitude. Especially girls. So I'd rather stick to 'acquaintances' for now."

Maddie was honestly surprised and slightly offended.

"So...are you trying to say popular people are bad...?" She said.

"No, not all of you. Your vibe just...gives me weird chills..."

"So...I'm...scary?"

"No, not like that. Not SCARY...just...unsettling"

"Unsettling....? And who are the not-bad popular people, if I may ask"

"W...well...I'm not sure, but there's a bunch of people-"

"Like Aiden? You two seem pretty 'acquaintanted'"

Marin stilled.

"...what...?"

"Yeah. I saw you two in that part of school no one goes to. You too seemed pretty...close"

"....w-well...were friends...'

"Friends dont stare at each other that they want to devour each other."

Marin’s cheeks flushed.

“That’s inappropriate…”

“Not really. I’m just describing what I saw. If I were you, I wouldn’t fall for Aiden. He’s…well, according to the things I’ve heard… he can be a really suffocating partner”

”a suffocating partner?”

“Yeah. My friend dated him once, and she just couldn’t keep up with him. He moves from one idea to the other too quickly.”

“He has ADHD, what did she expect? It’s not his fault. If she wanted calm, she should’ve dated Ethan instead”

“I guess you have a point there, but-“

“Sorry, I have to go. I don’t think Ethan’s coming today.” Marin said abruptly, getting up from his chair.

“So soon?” Maddie asked, cocking her head to the side a bit.

“Yeah. I’ve got homework, and your shadow’s moving on its own. It’s giving me the stink eye. I can tell” he continued quietly as he gathered his stuff.

As Marin was about to leave, Maddie suddenly spoke up

“Wait. What’re you like…INTO? Gender wise and species wise”

“Nothing. I’m aroace” Marin simply replied

He was aroace.

He’d never felt true attraction before.

At least, he doesn’t REMEMBER feeling it at all.

“Aroace? What a shame, I have thought you’d be bisexual or something”

“Well-“ Marin then realized.

Maddie didn’t want him to leave.

She was STALLING.

And he was running LATE.

“I have to go, if you want to talk to me…it’s not that hard to find me. See you tomorrow, Maddie.” Marin said before leaving.
